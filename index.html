Escalation Assessment (On-call Activation)
Complete Outage: Client cannot operate because core services or any critical dependency they rely on are unavailable. This could be all Bridgewise services down, or a single key API/endpoint/widget failing in a way that fully blocks their workflow.

Partial Degradation: Core services are available but some functionality is impaired (e.g., only some widgets, an endpoint variant, or a subset of actions respond). Client can work, but with reduced capability.

Minor / Cosmetic: Core workflows function; issues are visual/UI/translation quirks or isolated behavior that doesn't impact the client's core business outcomes.
Incorrect: Wrong values/signals present (higher risk impact). Missing: Coverage gaps; severity depends on scope. No data issue: Service/UI related only.
Use the broadest scope observed at the client (multi-universe ≈ hundreds of tickers; group ≈ handful of companies).
